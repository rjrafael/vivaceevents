User Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data stored by Vivace Events at any time. We take your privacy seriously and will process all deletion requests promptly.

What data we store
  • Your name and email address (from sign-in)
  • Profile information you provide (roles, skills, bio)
  • Gigs and opportunities you post or respond to
  • Groups and venues you create
  • Login provider info (Google, Facebook, or email)
How to request deletion

Send an email to rafael.javadov@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days and send a confirmation email once complete.

You may also delete your account directly from within the Vivace Events app at any time under Profile → Settings. This will immediately and permanently remove all your data from our servers.
